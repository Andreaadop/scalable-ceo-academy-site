The CEO Blueprint™

Three levers. Every bottleneck
traces back to one of them.

The CEO Blueprint™ is the organizing framework for everything inside the Academy. Before you start, a short diagnostic tells you which lever to pull first.

01
Lever One

Clarity

Most owners are working hard in every direction at once. Clarity is about defining exactly where you're going, what role you actually play, and what your business needs from you as CEO — not as a technician.

  • CEO role definition
  • Vision and 12-month priorities
  • Identifying your personal bottleneck
  • Revenue model clarity
02
Lever Two

Structure

Without operational infrastructure, growth just means more chaos. Structure is where you build the systems, processes, and team design that let work get done without running through you.

  • SOP development and documentation
  • Org design and reporting lines
  • Estimating and job management systems
  • Financial dashboards and KPIs
03
Lever Three

Leadership

Systems alone don't scale people. Leadership covers the transition from hands-on operator to someone who leads, delegates, hires well, and builds a team that performs without constant supervision.

  • Hiring and onboarding frameworks
  • Accountability and performance culture
  • Promoting from within — the SIC role
  • CEO-level decision making
